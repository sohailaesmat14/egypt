my favourite places in Egypt

there aare lots of places to visit in egypt

the pyramids

The pyramids of Giza are monumental tombs built for Egypts ancient pharaohs,
standing as a testament to their advanced engineering and architectural
Among them, the Great Pyramid of Khufu is the last surviving wonder of the ancient world.

khan elkhalili

Khan El Khalili, a historic bazaar in Cairo,
is a treasure trove of Egyptian culture, offering traditional crafts,
jewelry, and spices.Its narrow alleys and vibrant atmosphere transport visitors to the heart of Cairo's rich heritage.
